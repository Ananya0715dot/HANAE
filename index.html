About the Company

Founded by Ananya Sharma, CEO and Founder, HANAE International Pvt. Ltd. is a premium Korean beauty care brand established in 2022 and headquartered at the prestigious Mindspace One BKC, Mumbai. The company imports high-quality skincare products from South Korea and exports them to Australia, New Zealand, and select European countries. Our mission is to bring authentic, effective, and luxurious Korean skincare to global consumers.

Meet the Founder

Ananya Sharma, the driving force behind HANAE, brings a vision of pure beauty and global quality. Her passion for skincare and innovation led to the foundation of a brand that combines Korean science with natural purity. Ananya believes every person deserves radiant, healthy skin — and HANAE is her way of making that possible.
